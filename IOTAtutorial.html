IOTA Node Tutorial

This is a tutorial to run a node on windows

Posted by Necropaz on June 8, 2016
70% Complete

Step 1 - Download

Here you can download your IOTA client for your System. (click on the symbol on the left side)

Install the client after downloading.

Step 2 - Login

After you install your IOTA Wallet you can start it and login with your seed.

Your seed is your password and only letters and 9's are allowed.

Step 3 - Nodes

For claiming IOTAs it is recommendet to have 7 Nodes. You can ask for Nodes IP here:

Chat or Forum

After you collect 7 Nodes IP you can go to Tools and then click on Edit Server Nodes.

Step 4 - Save Nodes

Now Here you can enter the Nodes IP you recived in the following style:

+udp://IPADRESS:14265

After you have entered all your Nodes IP you can click on save and the IOTA client will restart automatic.

Step 5 - Claim

After Step 4 the IOTA client will restart automatic and you have to reenter your Seed an login. (See Step 2).

Now you can click on Claim (as you can see in the picture).

Step 6 - Donate

Here you can enter the precent of your IOTAs which you will spend to the foundation.

If you will not donate everything or later then tip 0 and click on CLAIM IOTA.

Now you have to wait a long time. (Claiming need about one hour per 1 Ti)

Step 7 - Check Claiming

On the Tap History you can check your if your claiming was succescfull.

Remeber it tooks much time to claim.

Step 8 -Server Log

To check your syncing you have to click on Tool and then on Server Log

Step 9 - Check syncing

Now Here you can see sometimes: Transacions to request = X / YYYY ( Z ms)

If YYYY is bigger than 31'000 your client is fully synced.
